Amader Gram Cancer Care and Research Center (AGCRC)

Electronic Medical Record (EMR) System

Guidelines

  • Step 1: Register your account by providing accurate information. If you are a new patient, click "New," and if you are an old patient, click "Follow-up."
  • Step 2: Your information will be shown on the List of Patients page after completing the registration.
  • Step 3: Click "View" on the corresponding patient name to go to the Health History page.
  • Step 4: Submit Patients required Health History.
  • Step 5: Then, Take Determinants of Health and Medical History.
  • Step 6: Finally, see your information on the EMR Page.
  • Step 7: After that select particular or multiple disease select and take relevant input.
  • Step 8: Update previous information if needed.
  • Step 9: Submit diseases to the EMR.
  • Step 10: Submitted!
