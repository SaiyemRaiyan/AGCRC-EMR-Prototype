Red flags: Trauma history, acute history, fever, protracted nausea/vomiting. Absence of flatus. Pain which is really lower chest pain with pain that is squeezing crushing and intense pain brought on by exertion. Spasmodic, sharp pain in flank/abdomen which radiates to groin. Weight loss. Dark stools or rectal bleeding.
